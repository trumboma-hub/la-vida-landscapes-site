A Long-Term Vision

Vida Gardens.
A vision taking root.

Therapeutic gardens for healing. An attached nursery. A sensory-gardens nonprofit serving the Southeast. The work La Vida is moving toward, one season at a time.

The Vision

Gardens that don’t just look beautiful. Gardens that help people heal.

Founder Triston Parsons originally enrolled at UAB for occupational therapy. The instinct to help people recover, regulate, and find rhythm never left. Vida Gardens is what happens when that instinct meets a landscape company , sensory gardens designed alongside care providers, families, and patients themselves.

Four Pillars

What we’re building toward.

  • Pediatric Wellness

    Sensory gardens designed alongside pediatric therapists. Tactile, regulating, and safe spaces for children with sensory processing needs.

  • Geriatric Wellness

    Memory gardens and accessible outdoor rooms designed with senior living communities, tested by the residents who use them.

  • Sensory Gardens Nonprofit

    A 501(c)(3) building public sensory gardens across the Southeast, free for the families who need them most.

  • Therapeutic Nursery

    An attached nursery growing the specimen plants Vida Gardens specifies, closing the loop from seed to installed garden.

Roadmap

A long path, walked one step at a time.

  1. 1

    Today

    Horticultural therapy installs

    Therapeutic gardens delivered as part of La Vida Landscapes residential and institutional projects. Today’s work feeds tomorrow’s playbook.

  2. 2

    Near-term

    Vida Gardens nursery launch

    Attached nursery in the Birmingham metro, growing the specimen plants therapeutic gardens depend on.

  3. 3

    Mid-term

    Sensory Gardens nonprofit launch

    501(c)(3) status, partnerships with pediatric and senior care institutions, and the first publicly-accessible Vida Garden.

  4. 4

    Long-term

    Franchise & multi-state expansion

    La Vida Landscapes and Vida Gardens, integrated as a franchisable model, planted in Huntsville, Smith Lake, and beyond.

Partner with Us

Care provider, donor, or builder. We’d love to talk.

Vida Gardens is a long arc and we’re still in chapter one. If you run a pediatric clinic, a senior-living community, a foundation, or you simply care about gardens that heal , we’d love to find a way to build with you.
