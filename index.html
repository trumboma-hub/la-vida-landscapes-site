    § 04

    Our Process

    From sketch
    to soil.

    No handoffs, no finger-pointing. One drawing set, one install schedule, one warranty.

    1. —— 01

      Walk the property

      Thirty minutes on-site, on us. We listen, sketch, and tell you honestly whether we are the right fit.

    2. —— 02

      Design & sketch

      Site plans, planting schemes, and 2D/3D renderings — signed off by ownership before a shovel moves.

    3. —— 03

      Build & install

      Our crews carry the project end-to-end. Same hands from week one to walk-through.

    4. —— 04

      Light & irrigate

      Smart irrigation, low-voltage layered lighting, and any pool or putting-green integration.

    5. —— 05

      Steward

      A maintenance program tuned to your property keeps the install thriving for years to come.

    § 05 Our Story

    Started with 15 lawns
    and a knock on a door.

    Triston Parsons started La Vida in high school with a push mower and a stubborn faith. A pivot from occupational therapy at UAB into full-time design-build came when one client asked if he could handle more than maintenance — and the answer became the company.

    “I didn’t have any systems and was taking on a lot of work. The lack of systems was hurting me. So I built them.”

    — Triston Parsons, founder
