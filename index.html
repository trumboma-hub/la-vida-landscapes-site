Our Story

Started with 15 lawns and a knock on a door.

Triston Parsons started La Vida in high school with a push mower and a stubborn faith. A pivot from occupational therapy at UAB into full-time design-build came when one client asked if he could handle more than maintenance — and the answer became the company.

“I didn’t have any systems and was taking on a lot of work. The lack of systems was hurting me. So I built them.” — Triston Parsons, founder
